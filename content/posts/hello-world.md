Welcome to my blog! This is my first post, and I'm excited to start sharing my thoughts and experiences here.

## Why start a blog?

I've been thinking about starting a blog for a while now. There are a few reasons:

1. **Document my learning** - Writing helps me solidify concepts and remember things better
2. **Share knowledge** - If something I learned helps someone else, that's a win
3. **Build in public** - I want to share my projects and get feedback

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- Software engineering topics
- Side projects I'm working on
- Tools and workflows that make me more productive
- Lessons learned from my work
